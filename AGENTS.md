# Application Context and Rules

## Team Members and Roles
The application is designed for exactly six team members: Yogesh (Leader), Mithun (Co-Leader), Nishanth, Farhan, Renuga, and Gokul.
Leader and Co-Leader have full access to all profiles, photo galleries, targets, and task management features.
Members can access only their own dashboards and assigned tasks.

## Google Drive Integration
Each of the six team members must have a dedicated Google Drive folder under a common parent folder named "Team Photo Upload Tracker".
All uploads must be stored in the corresponding member’s folder.
Yogesh (Leader) and Mithun (Co-Leader) have full access to all folders and dashboards, while other members can access only their own folders and data.

## Features
- Progress goals at both a team-wide level and an individual level.
- Task management: Leaders can assign jobs/tasks to members, and members can mark them as completed.
- Direct-to-Drive uploading for large image support.
